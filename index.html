About Me

I am an Embedded Systems Engineer with a background in electronics and industrial engineering, seeking opportunities in electronics and embedded system development. My interests span several technology-driven sectors such as aeronautics, automotive systems, industrial electronics, and intelligent connected devices. I enjoy working on multidisciplinary projects where hardware and software interact to create reliable and efficient systems.

My experience includes the design and development of embedded systems combining hardware and software, from electronic board design (schematics, PCB routing, testing) to embedded programming on microcontrollers such as STM32 and ESP32. I have worked on projects involving communication protocols (I2C, UART, SPI, CAN), real-time systems, human–machine interfaces, and system integration. I am particularly motivated by building robust electronic systems that interact with sensors, actuators, and real-world environments in industrial and technological applications.
